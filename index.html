📌 OB Montessori Intramurals

Yearly activity under the Physical Education (PE) subject, every January to March

🎯 About the Activity

The OB Montessori Intramurals is often held from January to March to test students' collaboration and motor skills. Students from Grade 7 to 12 are assigned on four different teams: Green Hornets, Yellow Tigers, Red Bulldogs, and Blue Bears. Each team must collaborate to earn a high score and win as the Overall Champion on the culminating day of the activity!

The Intramurals showcases a variety of sports such as Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, and Dodgeball. In addition, it supports Filipino games like Kadang-Kadang, Hula Hoop Relay, and Tug of War.

The Intramurals of OB Montessori is also a staple for extracurricular achievement within athletics throughout the school year. It is one of the premier events of how OB Montessorians make the man or woman within themselves.
